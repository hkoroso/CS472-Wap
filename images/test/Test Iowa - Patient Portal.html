Recommendations
Stay at home: Most people with COVID-19 have mild illness and are able to recover at home without medical care. Do not leave your home, except to get medical care. Do not visit public areas.

You should seek medical care immediately if you are experiencing an emergency and share that you have recently been diagnosed with COVID-19. Your healthcare provider can also talk with you about whether therapeutic options are appropriate for you. Finally, don’t forget that vaccination is still important for people who have been infected with COVID-19 and is an important way to protect those around you. For more information on vaccines talk with your healthcare provider or visit: CDC - Your COVID-19 Vaccination.
